## Brief

Most interfaces are designed for one person. This project asks you to design an interface that requires two people. It should only "work" (produce the intended output) when both are present and engaged.

## Requirements

- The interface must require **two bodies** to function. 
- It should be clear when one person is missing or disengaged
- Consider the relationship between the two people: are they collaborating, competing, leading/following, calling/responding, or something else? Produce output that depends on that relationship.

## Questions to Consider

- What is the dynamic between the two people?
- Do they have the same role, or different?
- What happens when one person stops? Does the system break, pause, or change?
- Is there a goal, an arc, or is the interaction open-ended?
